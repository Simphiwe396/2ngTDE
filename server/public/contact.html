Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within South Africa. Express shipping is available for urgent orders.

We offer 30-day returns on all unused items. Fragrances must be sealed for returns.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently we ship within South Africa, with plans to expand internationally soon.
